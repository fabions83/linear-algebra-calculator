Welcome to Linear Algebra Plus, a software designed to help you perform common linear algebra problems with just a few clicks! Whether you're studying for an exam, working on a homework assignment, or have any other reason to solve linear algebra problems, Linear Algebra + provides you with just the right tools to perform quick and efficient operations on matrices of up to 20 dimensions.

Linear Algebra + can perform the following operations on matrices

  • Determinant of a square matrix
  • Matrix multiplication of two matrices
  • Row reduced echelon Form of a matrix
  • Inverse of a square matrix
  • Eigenvectors and eigenvalues of a square matrix
